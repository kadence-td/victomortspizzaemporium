Our Story

The Origin

In 1987, Victor opened a small pizza shop in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Armed with a stone oven, a family recipe, and a dream, he built something the city had never tasted before.

Decades later, Victomort's Pizza Emporium remains family owned, stone fired, and tastier than ever. Every pizza is made to order, every ingredient sourced locally, and every delivery made with the same dedication that started it all.
